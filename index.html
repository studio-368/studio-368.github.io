Traveler's Notebook

Monster Tales

ABOUT

Studio 368 consists of an interdisciplinary team of ten students representing Animation, Computer Science, Creative Writing, Philosophy, and Telecommunication majors and one faculty mentor. We work together in a studio space three times a week in two hour chunks, for one semester. Instead of dividing tasks based on a person's skill set, we instead encourage members of our team to work on a volunteer basis, choosing what parts of the process they would like to pursue. Tasks include writing copy, generating code, playtesting, debugging, designing font stacks and color palettes, creating art, etc.

We recommend at least a 4th grade reading level.

GOALS

Our three main goals for the semester were to create a game that:

  1. Promotes cultural empathy
  2. Incorporates narrative as the driving factor in gameplay
  3. Includes a technology component (digital or hybrid game design)

Our three secondary goals were to create a game that:

  1. Encourages cooperative play
  2. Supports a variety of reading levels
  3. Develops oratory skills
